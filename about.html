About Profanally

Profanally is a simple yet powerful tool that helps you say things better. Whether you're drafting a message for work, replying to a tough email, or just trying to sound more polished, Profanally transforms your words into more refined versions — all in a tone that suits your purpose.

Built by an independent developer, Profanally was created with one goal in mind: to help people express themselves clearly, professionally, and confidently. No complicated interfaces. No fluff. Just better phrasing when you need it.

Profanally supports multiple tones — such as Polite, Professional, Diplomatic, and Legal — with a straightforward design that's easy to use. Free users can start rephrasing right away, while Pro users unlock more tone options and an ad-free experience.

We don’t collect personal data or ask you to create an account. Your privacy and writing are yours to keep.
