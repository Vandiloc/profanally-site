Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I upgrade to Pro?
    A: Go to the app’s settings and select “Upgrade” to access all tone styles.
  • Q: I lost access to Pro. What should I do?
    A: Tap "Restore Purchases" in the app settings.
  • Q: How do I request a refund?
    A: Refunds are handled by the App Store or Play Store. Please request via your app store account.
